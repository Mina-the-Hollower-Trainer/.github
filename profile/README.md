# Mina the Hollower Trainer 2026

**Direct Overview**  
Trainer for Mina the Hollower. Manages Bones, health, abilities, and world progression on the cursed island. Compatible with 2026 release. PC.  

Master every hollow and challenge: infinite resources, unbreakable survival, and full exploration freedom. Hotkey menu for platforming and combat.

**Core Features**

| Feature                  | Hotkey    | Function                              | Notes                              |
|--------------------------|-----------|---------------------------------------|------------------------------------|
| Unlimited Bones          | F1       | Infinite currency and experience      | Full upgrades                      |
| God Mode                 | F2       | Invulnerability                       | No damage                          |
| Infinite Hollowing       | F3       | Unlimited burrow energy               | Free underground travel            |
| Max Combat Stats         | F4       | Extreme whip and sidearm power        | Boss domination                    |
| Instant Trinkets         | F5       | Spawn any gear and items              | Custom loadouts                    |
| Enemy Control            | F6       | Freeze or weaken foes                 | Safe paths                         |
| Map & Secret Reveal      | F7       | Show all areas and hidden paths       | Complete exploration               |
| Progress Skip            | F8       | Advance story or areas                | Fast completion                    |
| Save Manager             | F9       | Quick run states                      | Retry sections                     |
| Preset Builds            | F10      | Save character configurations         | Multiple styles                    |
